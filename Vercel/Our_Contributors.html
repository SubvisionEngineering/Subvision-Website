Partner Stories

Each of our partners contributes uniquely to our vision, helping us create a sustainable future for our oceans.

Ocean Wise

$2,000

Ocean Wise has been a pivotal partner in our journey, providing not just financial support but also a platform to share our vision. Their commitment to ocean conservation and education aligns perfectly with our mission.

As an Ocean Wise Youth Partner, we've had the opportunity to present at their networking dinners, connecting with like-minded innovators and conservationists.

Shared Goals:

  • Ocean conservation
  • Educational outreach
  • Sustainable technology development

Seymour

Parts & Mentorship

Seymour has provided invaluable support through both hardware contributions and expert mentorship. Their willingness to test our thrusters and provide industry insights has accelerated our development process.

This partnership represents a perfect synergy, as our ZIMA project complements their existing product line while pushing the boundaries of what's possible in marine robotics.

Shared Goals:

  • Technical innovation
  • Industry advancement
  • Collaborative development

MATE ROV

$1,500 - $2,000

The MATE ROV competition organizers have supported our journey through travel bursaries, enabling our team to participate in global competitions and showcase our innovations on an international stage.

This exposure has been crucial for our growth, allowing us to learn from peers and experts while demonstrating the potential of our technologies.

Shared Goals:

  • Educational excellence
  • Competitive innovation
  • Global collaboration

SFU Engineering Science Faculty

$8,000

The Engineering Science Faculty at SFU has been our most substantial financial supporter, providing critical funding that has enabled our core research and development activities.

Their investment in our team reflects their commitment to fostering innovation among engineering students. In return, we've proudly represented the program through high school presentations, inspiring the next generation of engineers.

Shared Goals:

  • Student development
  • Engineering excellence
  • Educational outreach

Chang Institute

Co-op Salary & Mentorship

The Chang Institute's support took the form of a co-op salary for a team member, allowing for dedicated, full-time development of SubVision during a critical semester.

Beyond financial support, their mentorship and industry connections have opened doors to new opportunities and perspectives, enriching our approach to innovation.

Shared Goals:

  • Professional development
  • Entrepreneurial growth
  • Sustainable innovation
