Industry Issues

Toxic Antifouling

At least 55,000 tonnes of microplastics are released from ship and yacht coatings each year. Proactive cleaning eliminates the need for toxic antifouling paint.

Carbon Emissions

Biofouling on hulls causes increased fuel usage which accounts for 1% of current global emissions. With frequent hull-cleaning, this can be prevented from day 1.

Invasive Species

Nearly 80% of invasive marine species transfer is caused by biofouling hulls. By keeping hulls perpetually clean, we’re protecting the ocean.
