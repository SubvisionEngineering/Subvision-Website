Why this matters

Biofouling is not just a maintenance problem. It affects fuel burn, coatings, emissions pressure, and ecosystem risk at the same time.

Subvision is pursuing a prevention-first maintenance story: stay ahead of growth early, reduce the pressure for heavier intervention later, and give operators a more modern path for keeping hulls clean.

Three connected issues

Lower drag. Less coatings pressure. Reduced invasive transfer risk.

The full breakdown of toxic antifouling, carbon emissions, and invasive species lives on the Technology page, where the problem framing can go deeper without slowing down the homepage.
