Innovating Underwater Robotics

Pioneering the future of autonomous underwater vehicles at Simon Fraser University

SUBVISION

Who We Are

SubVision Robotics is a student-led startup based at Simon Fraser University, pioneering innovation in the field of underwater robotics. Our interdisciplinary team brings together passionate students from engineering, computer science, business, and design.

We believe that sustainable innovation starts with bold ideas and fearless execution — and that's exactly what drives our flagship project.

What We Do

Subvision focuses on solve real-world maritime challenges with cutting-edge technology and hands-on collaboration. For instance, our current Project, ZIMA, which is an advanced Remotely Operated Vehicle (ROV) with a singular mission: to revolutionize hull cleaning in the maritime industry.

At SubVision, we’re not just building a robot — we’re building a team, a mission, and a sustainable future for marine innovation.

Our Vision

We hope to inspire the next generation of innovators and leaders in marine technology, fostering a culture of sustainability and collaboration. Through developing technological solutions for ocean conservation, we aim to create a positive impact on our marine ecosystems.
